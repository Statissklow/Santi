Drum Lessons for All Age Groups:

Beginners and Children

For young drummers and absolute beginners, Santino Scavelli offers a playful and structured introduction to the world of drums. With over 15 years of teaching experience, he knows how to motivate children and introduce them to the basics of drumming in an entertaining way. Students learn fundamental techniques, rhythm skills, and music reading to build a solid foundation for their musical journey.

Teenagers and Advanced Students

For teenagers and advanced students, Santino offers individualized lessons tailored to the specific needs and interests of each person. Students can expand their skills in various music styles such as rock, pop, jazz, and metal. Santino places great emphasis on the development of technical skills, musical expression, and playing in a band.

Adults and Returning Players

Adults and returning players are also warmly welcomed by Santino. Whether you are learning to play the drums for the first time or want to refresh your previous knowledge, the lessons are tailored to your goals and availability. Santino creates a relaxed and supportive learning atmosphere where you can fully develop your potential.

Customized Lessons

The drum lessons with Santino Scavelli are characterized by a customized approach. Each student receives a curriculum tailored to their needs and progress, focusing on both technical aspects of drumming and musical interpretation and creativity.

Why Santino Scavelli?

With a double degree in Trossingen and at the Pop Academy and a deep understanding of rhythmic cultures worldwide, Santino Scavelli brings unique expertise and experience to his teaching. His extensive training and his awards, including the Meinl Outstanding Performance Award 2023, make him an exceptional teacher. Santino combines technical precision with creative freedom and promotes both independent play and the fusion of musical cultures and sounds.
