Tambour Duo/Quartet

Born as a duo, now expanding their music with new sounds.

...Identities of the international lineup...

The "Tambour Quartet", born as a duo, now expands their music with new sounds. An earthy sound that oscillates between oriental tradition, jazz, and blues, reflecting the identities of its international lineup. Sometimes delicate and soft, sometimes strong and powerful, a bundle of different styles that are consistently and balancedly mixed together to explore new areas of music and cultures that ultimately have so much in common.
