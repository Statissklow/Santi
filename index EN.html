About Santino

Santino Scavelli is one of the most exciting newcomers in the world music scene. With his unique style and skill, he has already received several prestigious awards, including the Framedrum Award 2018, the YouTube Early Career Award 2019, and the Meinl Outstanding Performance Award 2023.

As a Musical Director at the National Theater Mannheim and through his collaborations with artists such as Anika Nilles, Zohar Fresco, and Andrea Piccioni, he has gained international recognition. His projects like the "Pulse Project" and "Pour les Amis" showcase his versatility and creative vision.

Lesson 

With a double degree in Trossingen (Drums and Latin Percussion) and at the Pop Academy (Oriental Percussion), Santino Scavelli has developed a deep understanding of rhythmic cultures worldwide.

With over 15 years of teaching experience in individual and group lessons, gathered at music academies and through workshop series across Germany, Santino Scavelli offers inspiring lessons for anyone wishing to dive into the world of rhythm.

Studio & Recording    

Santino Scavelli is a sought-after studio musician. In his studio, he records albums for renowned artists (Anika Nilles, Viktor) – whether it's percussion, drums, or both.

His custom-built hybrid set, which he calls the "Split-Set", is particularly in demand. It is ideal for organic-electronic beats as well as for folkloric music. See for yourself by clicking "More" below.
