Latin Percussion Lessons for All Ages:

Beginners and Children

For children and absolute beginners, Santino Scavelli offers a playful introduction to the world of Latin percussion. The lessons not only enhance rhythm and coordination but also boost self-confidence and teamwork among young musicians. Through simple and fun exercises, the basics of Latin percussion are taught, helping children develop a solid musical foundation.

Teenagers and Advanced Students

Teenagers and advanced students benefit from Santino Scavelli's specialized lessons tailored to the needs of European bands. Since Santino is not from a Latin American background, he has developed a unique style that harmonizes particularly well with Western pop and rock music. Students learn how to seamlessly integrate Latin percussion into various music styles and improve their skills in playing with drums and other instruments.

Adults and Returning Players

Adults and returning players looking to learn or refresh their Latin percussion skills will find an ideal teacher in Santino Scavelli. His lessons are flexible and tailored to individual goals and time availability. His focus on the interaction between percussion and other instruments is especially valuable, enabling students to perfect their musical expression and ensemble playing.

Customized Lessons

Latin percussion lessons with Santino Scavelli are tailored to each student's needs. Technical skills, musical interpretation, and creativity are the focus. With Santino's comprehensive training and his ability to blend different musical cultures, students gain a unique and enriching learning experience.

Why Santino Scavelli?

Santino Scavelli brings a unique advantage to his lessons with his specialization in Latin percussion for pop and Western music. His experience as a drummer and percussionist allows him to find innovative ways to integrate percussion into pop bands. His education and accolades, including the Meinl Outstanding Performance Award 2023, make him an exceptional teacher. He combines technical precision with creative freedom and encourages independent play as well as the blending of musical cultures.
